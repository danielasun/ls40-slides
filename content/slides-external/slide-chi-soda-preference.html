The $\chi^2$-test for independence also compares expected and observed counts. However, here there is no expected probabilities and the computation for the expected counts is based on the rows and columns totals:

$\textrm{Expected Count}=\frac{\textrm{Row total}\times\textrm{Column total}}{\textrm{Sample size}}$
