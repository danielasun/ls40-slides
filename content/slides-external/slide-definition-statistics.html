Statistics?

Statistics is the science of
collecting,
organizing,
presentating,
analyzing,
interpreting
and dealing with the uncertainty of
data
to assist in making more effective decisions. (So that we can make better, more informed and critical decisions about the world around us.)

Data are everywhere!
Regardless of your field, interests, lifestyle, etc., you will almost definitely have to make decisions based on data, or evaluate decisions someone else has made based on data.
Statistics is basically the science of how to deal with all those data, and what information can I extract from them.
Use to make predictions
key word here is "assist". It won't give you the right answer. It will help you choose one.
