Resampling p-value (chance model)

Notes on the Null Hypothesis parameter ($\pi$)
of the chance model:
Chance model $\neq$ "50/50 probability"
$\pi \in [0-1]$

A dog is trained to detect the scent of explosives. When presented in front of 3 bags, 15 out of 21 times $(\hat{p}=0.71)$ the dog successfully detected the bag containing the explosives.

Did the training have an effect on this dog or could this result be explained by random chance?
