Theory, $\chi^2$ test for association

Remember that the ditribution of the $\chi^2$ statistic depends of the degrees of freedom inherent to your data.

For a 2-ways contingency table, the number of degrees of freedom can be obtained by:

$df=(\textrm{Number of rows} − 1)\times(\textrm{Number of columns} − 1)$

For the soda study:
$df = (3-1)\times(3-1) = 4$
