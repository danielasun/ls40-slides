Mean Absolute Difference statistic

The mean of the absolute values of the differences between the means/median of the explanatory groups, referred as the MAD statistic, provides a measure of how far appart the sample means/medians are on average from each other.

$\mathrm{MAD}=\sum_{\substack{i,j \\ i < j}}^n \frac{\mid\bar{x_i}-\bar{x_j}\mid}{n}$
