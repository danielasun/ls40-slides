$\chi^2$ test for association

The statistics behind a $\chi^2$ can also be used to test the difference between two or more explanatory groups of a response variable with two or more categories.

A $\chi^2$-test for association (often called a $\chi^2$-test for independence) tests for an association between two or more categorical variables.
