Can dolphins communicate?

Null Hypothesis
Buzz is just guessing (his probability of choosing the correct button is 0.50) and he got really lucky in these 16 attempts.
$\pi=0.5$
Alternative hypothesis
Buzz is doing something other than just guessing (his probability of choosing the correct button is more than 0.50).
$\pi>0.5$

The very small p-value we obtained when simulating random chance means our evidence is strong enough to be considered statistically significant.

That is, we don't think the result of the experiment (15 out of 16 correct button choices by Buzz) happened by chance alone, but rather, something other than "random chance" was at play.
